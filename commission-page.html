COMMISSIONS

and how to order


Status: OPEN [Current opening slot: Winter 2022]


This page is for individual clients/personal commission only. If you are a corporate client, please disregard the status and time slot.






TYPE A // Bust-up CHF 70 | Halfbody CHF 100 | Fullbody CHF 130


This style is drawn in a more simple way. Simple or no background. Halfbody means waist/thighs up. Additional characters cost + CHF 70/100/130. Price may vary depending on the complexity of the design of your character or composition.

TYPE B // Bust-up CHF 140 | Halfbody CHF 180 | Fullbody CHF 220


This style is a rendered full illustration with more details and option for a more complex background (if chosen). Halfbody means waist/thighs up. Additional characters cost + CHF 140/180/220. Price may vary depending on the complexity of the design of your character or composition.

TYPE C // Fullbody CHF 30


Chibi style with simple or no background. Additional characters cost + CHF 30

TERMS OF SERVICE


  • - I have the rights to decline any projects.
  • - Slots will not be first come first serve.
  • - All payments are via paypal only. Prices are all in CHF. Final price will have an 5% cost due to paypal fee.
  • - All commissions are for personal-use only. Kindly let me know beforehand
    if you plan to use it for commercial-use.
  • - I accept downpayment for commercial commissions.
  • - Full payment upfront is needed before I start working on the sketch.
    After I sent you an invoice, please pay immediately at least after 2-3 days.
    I will cancel the commission if a week has passed without you paying.
  • - Subject that I will not be able to draw: furries, real people/realism, offensive themes, graphic NSFW

PROGRESS


  • 1. Fill out the form and email the request. (Please see the request form/the formatting of the email below.
    Please make sure to provide all the information that is required.)

  • 2. I will contact you in a week if the request is accepted.

  • 3. Once I have received your payment, I will start working on the sketch. During the sketch process, list all the changes you want me to change (please be clear about it). Sketch will not be revised the second time unless it’s some minor changes.

  • 4. I will notify and give you updates from time to time when doing your commission. Progress update points are the following: [1] sketch, [2] lineart, [3] finished product. NO DEADLINE. This is mainly for personal-use commissions. Further discussion is available when it comes to projects or commercial-uses comms (should be at least 2 month prior the deadline).

  • 5. Once your commission is done, you will receive a png file for preview and the original size PSD file.
